For Buyers

How do I find verified suppliers?

Use our supplier verification filters to find Gold Suppliers with verified business licenses, factory audits, and trade history. Look for the verified badge on supplier profiles.

What payment methods are safe?

We recommend using iBlue Secure Payment for protection. We accept:
  • • Letter of Credit (L/C)
  • • Telegraphic Transfer (T/T)
  • • Western Union
  • • iBlue Escrow Service

How do I request a sample?

Click "Request Sample" on any product page. Most suppliers provide samples for a fee, which may be refunded upon bulk order placement.

For Suppliers

How do I get verified as a supplier?

Submit your business license, tax registration, and factory photos. Our verification team will conduct an onsite or virtual audit within 5-7 business days.

What are the selling fees?

Basic listing is free. Premium features start at $999/year including verified status, priority ranking, and RFQ access. Transaction fees apply only for completed sales.

How to manage international orders?

Use our Trade Assurance system to manage orders, track payments, and handle shipping documentation. We provide Incoterms guidance and customs support.

How to Register

Step-by-step registration guide

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  2. Choose account type: Buyer or Supplier
  3. Enter email and create password
  4. Verify email address
  5. Complete company profile
  6. Submit verification documents (for suppliers)
  7. Wait for approval (24-48 hours)

Documents required for verification

  • Business registration certificate
  • Tax identification number
  • Bank account details
  • Company photos/video
  • Product catalog (optional)
