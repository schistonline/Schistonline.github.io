Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about supplier verification

How long does verification take?
Standard verification takes 24-48 hours after document submission. Express verification (24 hours) is available for premium suppliers.
Is verification mandatory for all suppliers?
Verification is not mandatory but highly recommended. Verified suppliers receive significantly more inquiries and buyer trust.
What happens if my verification is rejected?
You'll receive specific feedback on what needs correction. You can re-submit corrected documents at any time.
Is my business information kept confidential?
Yes. Only the verified badge and basic business name are public. Detailed documents are for internal verification only.
Do I need to renew verification annually?
Yes, verification requires annual renewal to ensure business information remains current.
Can international suppliers get verified?
Yes, we verify international suppliers using their local business registration and tax documents.
