Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying on BuyUganda

Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies by supplier and product. Each product listing shows the MOQ. You can also negotiate with suppliers for lower quantities.
How do I know if a supplier is trustworthy?
Look for verified badges, read customer reviews, check transaction history, and always use Trade Assurance for payment protection.
Can I negotiate prices with suppliers?
Yes! B2B transactions often involve negotiation. Use our chat system to discuss pricing, MOQ, and payment terms.
What is Trade Assurance?
Trade Assurance is our buyer protection program that secures your payment until you confirm receipt of goods as described.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary by supplier location and shipping method. Discuss delivery timelines with your supplier before ordering.
What if I receive damaged or wrong products?
Open a dispute immediately through your order page. Our team will investigate and help resolve the issue.
