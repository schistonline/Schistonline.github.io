Our Story

Founded in 2020, BuyUganda was born from a simple observation: Ugandan businesses struggled to find reliable suppliers and buyers in a fragmented market. Traditional trade channels were inefficient, trust was hard to establish, and digital transformation was lagging behind.

Today, BuyUganda has grown into Uganda's leading B2B marketplace, connecting thousands of businesses across the country. We've facilitated millions of transactions and continue to innovate, bringing transparency, efficiency, and trust to B2B trade.
