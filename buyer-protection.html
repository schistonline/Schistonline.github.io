What's Covered

Buyer Protection covers the following situations

Item Not Received

If you paid but never received your order, we'll refund your full payment.

Not as Described

Products that significantly differ from the listing description qualify for refund.

Late Delivery

If your order arrives significantly later than promised delivery date.

Damaged Goods

Products that arrive damaged or defective are covered for replacement/refund.

Wrong Quantity

If you receive fewer items than ordered, we'll help get the difference.

Supplier Fraud

Full protection against fraudulent suppliers on our platform.

How to File a Claim

Follow these steps to submit a buyer protection claim

1

Contact the Supplier First

Reach out to the supplier through our messaging system to resolve the issue directly.

2

Open a Dispute

If unresolved within 3 days, open a dispute from your order details page.

3

Provide Evidence

Upload photos, videos, or documentation supporting your claim.

4

Our Team Reviews

Our dispute resolution team reviews all evidence within 2-3 business days.

5

Resolution

We'll issue a refund if your claim is approved, or help facilitate a solution.

Important Notes: Buyer Protection applies to eligible transactions only. Claims must be filed within 15 days of order delivery. Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days after claim approval. Custom and made-to-order items have modified protection terms.
