Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I become a supplier?

Register an account, complete verification, and start listing your products. Our team will guide you through the process.

How do I place an order?

Browse products, contact suppliers for quotes, negotiate terms, and place your order through our secure platform.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Pesapal (cards & mobile money), bank transfers, and mobile money (MTN/Airtel).

How long does verification take?

Supplier verification typically takes 24-48 hours after submitting all required documents.

Do you offer delivery?

Delivery is arranged between buyers and suppliers. We partner with trusted logistics providers.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant payment gateways.
