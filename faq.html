Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BuyUganda marketplace

What is BuyUganda?
BuyUganda is Uganda's premier B2B marketplace connecting verified suppliers with serious buyers across East Africa. We facilitate wholesale trade, bulk purchases, and business-to-business transactions.
Is BuyUganda free to use?
Basic membership is completely free for both buyers and suppliers. Suppliers can upgrade to paid plans for additional features like unlimited listings, featured products, and priority support.
How do I create an account?
Click the "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your basic information, verify your email, and complete your profile. The process takes less than 2 minutes.
Do I need to verify my business?
Business verification is optional but highly recommended. Verified suppliers receive a trust badge, higher search rankings, and significantly more buyer inquiries. Buyers also benefit from verified supplier status.
How do I find products on BuyUganda?
Use the search bar to look for specific products, browse categories, or filter by supplier type, price range, and location. You can also request quotes by posting your requirements.
Can I request samples before bulk purchase?
Yes! Most suppliers offer samples for quality verification. Contact the supplier directly through our messaging system to arrange sample orders.
How do I know if a supplier is trustworthy?
Look for the verified badge, check supplier ratings and reviews, review their transaction history, and always use Trade Assurance for payment protection on large orders.
What is Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ is the minimum number of units a supplier requires for a single order. This varies by supplier and product. You can negotiate MOQ directly with suppliers through our chat system.
Can I negotiate prices with suppliers?
Absolutely! B2B transactions often involve negotiation. Use our chat system to discuss pricing, MOQ, payment terms, and delivery arrangements.
How do I become a seller on BuyUganda?
Click "Become a Supplier" on the homepage, complete the registration form, submit your business documents, and start listing your products. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours.
What documents are required for supplier verification?
You'll need a valid business registration certificate, tax identification number (TIN), director/owner ID, proof of business address, and company bank account details.
How much does it cost to sell on BuyUganda?
Basic membership is free with limited listings. Paid plans start from UGX 99,000/month for unlimited listings, featured products, and premium features. See our Pricing page for details.
How do I get the verified supplier badge?
Complete the supplier verification process by submitting all required business documents. Once verified, you'll receive the trust badge automatically.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), Bank Transfers, and Escrow payments through our Trade Assurance program.
What is Trade Assurance?
Trade Assurance is our buyer protection program that secures your payment until you confirm receipt of goods as described. It covers product quality, shipping time, and supplier fraud.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with PCI DSS standards. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.
How long does refund processing take?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after claim approval. The time to reflect in your account depends on your payment provider.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary by supplier location and shipping method. Domestic deliveries typically take 2-7 days, while international shipping can take 2-6 weeks. Discuss delivery timelines with your supplier.
Who pays for shipping?
Shipping costs can be negotiated with the supplier. Some suppliers offer free shipping on large orders, while others charge based on weight, volume, or destination.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Once shipped, suppliers provide tracking numbers. You can monitor delivery status in your order dashboard.
What if my order is damaged or lost?
Open a dispute immediately through your order page. Our team will investigate and help resolve the issue, including refunds or replacements if covered by Trade Assurance.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
How do I update my profile information?
Log in to your account, go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings" to update your personal information, business details, contact information, and preferences.
Can I delete my account?
Yes. Contact our support team to request account deletion. Please note that certain information may be retained for legal and transactional purposes.
How do I change my email address?
Go to Account Settings, select "Change Email," verify your current password, and enter your new email address. You'll receive a verification link to confirm the change.
